Aloha!

I’m Cassidy, a college student born and raised on the Big Island of Hawai’i currently studying in Seattle.

Growing up, I loved exploring ways to express myself artistically- whether through drawing, photography, filmmaking, or graphic design. As I got older, I began to take notice of the design of products and systems I interacted with everyday. The way a design shapes people’s experiences- from advertisements to buildings- has always fascinated me.

After moving to Seattle for college and taking my first computer science class- I became interested in the intersection of design, computing, and human-computer interaction.

When I’m not studying or doing social media & graphic design for campus organizations, you can find me taking photos of friends, learning new sports, or out in nature!
